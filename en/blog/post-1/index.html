Second mviewer event in Paris

The Steering Committee (PSC) is pleased to announce the upcoming second mviewer meeting in Paris (15th arrondissement) on November 25 at the Place de la transformation publique.

Provisional program

10:00 - Welcome and introduction by the PSC

10:10 - Getting to know mviewer and its users

Introduction to mviewer - Meet the community - Use cases: demos / flash presentations

12:h30 - Lunch break - (! the meal will not be taken in charge by the organizers)

13:30 - The mviewer factory (workshops)

16:30 - End
